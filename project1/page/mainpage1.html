color recognition

Teaching children colors is one of the basic skills a child learns. The importance of teaching children colors at an early age helps the child communicate with the outside world. By describing people, and describing nature with its wonderful colors. Learning the names of colors helps develop the child's vocabulary, and learn to sort things according to their color. It is useful to know the colors to indicate danger, such as red in a traffic light to stop. Helps develop a child's mind through organization, classification,
and comparison. It helps develop the child's skills by learning counting and numbers.
